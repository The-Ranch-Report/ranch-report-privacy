# Privacy Policy for The Ranch Report

**Effective date:** May 20, 2026
**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Austin Cato ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use The Ranch Report mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

If you have questions, contact us at **the.ranch.report1@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to make the App work. The categories of information collected are:

### a. Location
- **What:** Your approximate device location, used to show nearby restaurants on the map.
- **When:** Only while you are actively using the App. We never collect location in the background.
- **Stored:** Location is processed in-memory on your device and not stored on our servers.

### b. Photos
- **What:** Photos you choose to attach to a ranch dressing review.
- **When:** Only when you tap "Add Photo" and pick a photo from your library.
- **Stored:** Photos are stored on your device and, if you save a community rating, in our cloud database (Google Firebase).

### c. Account Information
- **What:** A unique anonymous user identifier (UID) assigned by Firebase Authentication. If you choose to sign in with Apple, we may receive your name and email (or Apple's private relay email, depending on your choice).
- **Why:** To associate your ratings with your account so you can edit or delete them.

### d. User-Generated Content
- **What:** Your restaurant ratings (1–10 score), written reviews, ranch type selection, and any photos you attach.
- **Why:** To display your contributions to the community and let other users see ranch ratings.
- **Visibility:** Other users of the App can see your ratings, reviews, photos, and username associated with each rating.

### e. Username
- **What:** A display name you choose (default: a random nickname like "RanchFan123").
- **Why:** Shown alongside your community ratings.

### f. Product Interaction
- **What:** Anonymous interactions with the App (e.g., which screens were viewed, crash reports).
- **Why:** To understand how the App is used and to fix bugs.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App's core features (map, ratings, reviews).
- Display community ratings and reviews to other users.
- Allow you to create, edit, and delete your own ratings.
- Diagnose and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
- Comply with legal obligations.

**We do not sell your personal information.** We do not use your information for advertising or for purposes other than operating the App.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these limited cases:

- **With other App users:** Your ratings, reviews, photos, and username are visible to other users of the App.
- **Service providers:** The third-party services listed above process data on our behalf.
- **Legal obligations:** If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety and rights of others.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Local data:** Stays on your device until you uninstall the App.
- **Community ratings:** Stored in our cloud database until you delete the rating or your account.
- **Account data:** Stored as long as your account exists. You may request account deletion at any time (see Section 7).

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Correct inaccurate information.
- **Deletion:** Request that we delete your account and associated data.
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format.
- **Withdraw consent:** Disable location access in iOS Settings at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **the.ranch.report1@gmail.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you additional rights. If you are in the EU/UK, the GDPR applies. We honor these rights for all users regardless of location.
